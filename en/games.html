NoTemp'o

Developer Manager • AI Programmer • End-of-Bachelor Project

NoTemp’o is a detective/infiltration game set in an alternative version of 22nd century Italy. With a team of 16 people, we developed a vertical slice featuring the game's third level over 8 months using Unity.
NoTemp'o won the 2023 Best Video Game Award from e-artsup schools.

  • Engine: Unity
  • Language: C#
  • Team: 4 Developers • 7 Artists • 4 Voice Actors • 1 Composer

Santa Claws

Developer • Game Jam

Game jam project developed in 15 days by a team of two on the theme of "Christmas". Santa Claws is an arcade game where the player must deliver presents on Christmas Eve.
Inspired by Christmas tales and the Brothers Grimm fairy tales, the assets were created with hand-cut colored paper photographs giving the game a puppet-theatre aesthetic.
